Common questions

Why pay if the open-source version is free? ▼

Self-hosting is great if you're a developer. The hosted plan gives you a working URL the same day, persistent cloud cache, scheduled snapshots, and a dedicated IP so Instagram doesn't rate-limit you as aggressively. Plus you support continued development.

Do I need to provide my own AI key? ▼

On Free: yes — bring your own Gemini, Groq, OpenAI, or Claude key. Gemini and Groq both have free tiers with no credit card. On Pro and Team: optional — you can still BYO key for unlimited usage, or use Pulse's included monthly AI budget (~500 generations on Pro, ~2000 on Team).

What about my data? ▼

Free: everything stays on your machine. Pro/Team: data lives in your own isolated tenant on our infrastructure. We never sell data. We never train on your data. See the Privacy Policy.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes. Cancel in one click — you keep access until the end of the billing period. After that you fall back to Free (self-host).
