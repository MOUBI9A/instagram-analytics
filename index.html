        Drop your Instagram export

        Everything runs locally in your browser — your data never leaves this device. Both JSON and HTML export formats work. Drop the .zip Instagram emailed you.

        Large export? Paste the full path to the zip on your Mac — the local Python server will stream-parse it without browser memory limits.

        💡 In Finder, right-click the zip → Hold ⌥ Option → Copy <name> as Pathname, then paste.

        How to request your Instagram export (2 min, then 1–48h wait)
        1. In the Instagram app: Settings → Centre de comptes (Accounts Center).
        2. Vos informations et autorisations → Télécharger vos informations.
        3. Pick your account → Quelques-unes de vos informations or Toutes.
        4. Format: JSON (preferred, smaller) or HTML — both work. Quality: any. Date range: Depuis le début.
        5. Submit. Instagram emails you a download link within 1–48 hours.
        6. Download the .zip, drop it above.
